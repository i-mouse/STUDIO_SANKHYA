Metropolitan Melody

Discover the artistry of modern living with Modern Home Interior Designers in Dwarka.We create spaces that blend style and function, transforming homes into elegant, comfortable sanctuaries tailored to your lifestyle.

Development

The client desired simplicity and a contemporary open atmosphere for their home. As Eco-Friendly Architects for Residential Projects in South Delhi, we introduced a front garden and a green strip alongside the house, enhancing the limited green spaces in the metropolitan setting. Situated in a residential area away from the city's high-rises, this location allowed Renovation Architects in Dwarka to seamlessly integrate sustainable design elements into the overall vision, creating a tranquil yet modern living space.

Design

To achieve a modern allure, Renovation Architects in Dwarka incorporated wooden planks beneath the balcony ceiling, paired with sleek black MS railings and glass on the facade. This combination exudes sophistication while maintaining elegance. To enhance the visual appeal further, Modern Home Interior Designers in Dwarka introduced subtle lighting between the wooden planks and the parapet wall, creating a captivating floating effect for the cantilever, adding a striking modern touch to the homeâ€™s design.

Idea and Concept

The design concept focused on delivering a modern, contemporary aesthetic while emphasizing natural light, ventilation, and scenic green views to suit the client's preferences. By embracing Modern Architecture in South Delhi, the project prioritizes open spaces and seamless indoor-outdoor connections, creating a home that feels light, airy, and intimately connected with its surroundings.

  • Area: 2451 Sq.ft.
  • Location: Gurugram, Haryana, India
