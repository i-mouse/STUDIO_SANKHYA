NaturaVista Haven

Experience a seamless blend of nature and design with Eco-Friendly Architects for Residential Projects in South Delhi. We integrate sustainable practices with innovative design to create homes that harmonize with the environment while offering modern elegance.

Development

Surrounded by bungalows, this property initially lacked greenery both inside and out. To create a nature-infused space for connection, the family embraced a terrace garden with a water element. This strategic addition, part of our Custom Home Designs in South West Delhi, provides a refreshing escape from the heat and enhances the overall living experience.

Design

This terrace garden embodies an authentic connection to nature, incorporating natural stone, wood, and metal. As part of our Sustainable Architecture in Dwarka, the combination of these materials, along with natural grass and a water feature, creates a serene and visually calming ambiance. The strategic use of light and dark-colored stones enhances the illusion of a space intimately connected to the beauty of nature, reflecting our commitment to sustainable and harmonious design.

Idea and Concept

Faced with the challenge of limited space, our Interior Design Studio in South Delhi conceptualized and proposed a terrace garden, offering a distinctive home oasis. This innovative solution fulfilled the client's wish for a garden and introduced a novel way for the family to immerse themselves in nature, creating a serene haven on their terrace.
Project Details

  • Area: 3150 sq.ft.
  • Location: South Delhi, India
