Explore how our approach to modern architecture can redefine your living experience, blending cutting-edge aesthetics with practical sophistication.

Development

The client desired simplicity and an open, contemporary atmosphere for their home. As Eco-Friendly Architects for Residential Projects in South Delhi, we embraced the challenge of incorporating green elements into a metropolitan setting with limited natural spaces. By introducing a front garden and a green strip alongside the house, we harmonized the design with its residential surroundings. The location, nestled away from the city's high-rises, provided the ideal backdrop for realizing our vision of sustainable and aesthetically pleasing architecture.

Design

To achieve a modern allure, the Best Architecture Firm in Dwarka utilized wooden planks beneath the balcony ceiling, complemented by sleek black MS railings with glass elements in the building's facade. This design choice not only upholds an elegant aesthetic but also enhances sophistication. To further elevate the house's visual appeal, we incorporated subtle lighting between the wooden planks and the parapet wall, creating a captivating floating effect for the cantilever.

Idea and Concept

The design concept, crafted by the Best Architecture Firm in Dwarka, aimed to deliver a modern, contemporary aesthetic with a focus on abundant natural light, effective ventilation, and scenic green views. This approach was carefully tailored to align with the client's preferences, ensuring a living space that is both visually striking and functionally enriching.
Project Details

  • Area: 2451 Sq.ft.
  • Location: Gurugram, Haryana, India
