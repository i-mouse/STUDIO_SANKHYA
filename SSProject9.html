Luxe Haven

This duplex project perfectly blends luxury materials, functionality, and personalized spaces, creating a harmonious home where each element contributes to a modern yet comfortable living environment.

Development

The family desired a home that would reflect their modern taste while ensuring that each member had their own distinct space. The challenge was to balance individual needs with a unifying design element that would bring cohesion to the overall layout. Mirrors played a key role in making the spaces feel larger and brighter, while the use of stone and glass created a sleek, contemporary ambiance. Wooden furniture added warmth, ensuring that the modern elements were balanced by comfort and livability.

Design

The design incorporated mirror work throughout the duplex to visually expand the space and reflect light, giving the home a bright, open feel. Stone materials, such as marble and granite, were used in both flooring and accent walls to add a touch of luxury, while wooden furniture brought warmth and texture to the interiors. Metallic frames were introduced in furniture design, adding a modern touch to key pieces like coffee tables, dining chairs, and shelving units, giving them depth and a sense of elegance.

Idea and Concept

The primary concept behind the design was to create an open, expansive feel in the duplex through the use of mirrors and glass elements, while ensuring each room had its own identity. Stone was chosen to introduce a luxurious and grounded element, contrasting with the lighter, more airy feel of the glass and mirrors. Wooden furniture was incorporated to soften the modern materials, ensuring a comfortable and inviting living environment. The metallic frames added a layer of sophistication and modernity, providing depth to the furniture and enhancing the overall aesthetic of the home.
Project Details

  • Area: 2250 sq ft
  • Location: South West Delhi

This duplex interior project for a family of eight showcases a blend of luxury, functionality, and personalized space. Featuring extensive use of mirror work, stone materials, glass walls, and wooden furniture, the design balances modern elegance with comfort. Metallic frames were introduced to add depth and sophistication, accentuating the furniture and creating visual interest.

Each room was tailored to meet individual needs while maintaining a cohesive design language throughout the home, bringing together different materials for a rich, layered aesthetic.
